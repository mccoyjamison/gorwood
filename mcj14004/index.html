Record Management Services

Electronic Record Management

Document Scanning, Document Capture and Management are some of the Electronic Records Management services that we offer. Gorwood does the scanning, indexing, document conversion and data capture either off-site or onsite for clients looking for Electronic Document Management.

Physical Record Management

Whether clients have active or inactive documents and feel the need for additional space, better security, confidentiality, accessibility, compliance with local and global regulations and long term preservation of documents, Gorwood’s Document Storage, File Storage and Management Services will be the answer.

Multimedia Storage

Gorwood offers world class effective media archival ensuring backup video and data security. Whether clients have SLR data cartridges, 4mm, 8mm, DLT, SDLT tapes, cassettes or other forms of media, our multimedia storage service will ensure backup media are secure, well preserved and immediately available when needed.

Document Destruction

Whether you're a small, medium or large company looking for document destruction services to protect privacy and confidentiality or need to safely destroy specific documents, Gorwood keeps your confidential information secure.

Microfilm and Imaging Services

Gorwood digitizes and indexes Aperture Cards, Microfilm, and Microfiche. This process allows the data, currently accessed by aging and obsolete readers, to be accessed by multiple users from modern devices.
